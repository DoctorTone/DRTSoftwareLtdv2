Covid-19 in the UK

Visualising the Covid-19 Pandemic

The current coronavirus epidemic has generated vast quantities of data, forming the basis for the scientific decisions on lockdown and social distancing and allowing for public scrutiny of the information. This visualisation separates out the main strands of data for each day and allows them to be compared or studied in isolation. The application also highlights how completely separate visualisations can be incorporated by switching between subsets of the data.
