Services

Let us create an experience for you.

Defining your experience

We can create all kinds of 3D experiences for you - from visualising your data to creating a virtual world for you to explore. However, our most important service is ensuring that you end up with the application that you want. Do you have data to visualise but no idea how to do it or what you'd like it to look like? Do you want to create a virtual space for training in a safe environment, visualise a building interior, interact with medical data but don't know where to start? No problem! We can do as much or as little as you like.

How do we do it?

During our initial consultation we gather all your requirements and determine all the functionality that you'd like to see in your application. We take into account how you'd like the experience to look and what features it needs to have. Whether you have a list of exact requirements or want to leave all the design to us the process is similar each time. We endeavour to get an initial prototype up and running quickly to ensure that we are on the right track. We then keep communicating with you throughout the process to ensure you are happy at each step (and encourage you to do the same). We review progress regularly ensuring that you are happy with the direction of the project. We do not stop until the project has been reviewed and you are completely happy with all aspects.

What we offer

We can build any type of 3D application for you. We specialise in the following:

  • Data Visualisation
  • Virtual Reality
  • Augmented Reality
If you would like some help in creating any other type of application then please get in touch.
