Drum Visualisation

Visualisations take many forms. They are not necessarily about bar charts and graphs. This demo visualises how a collection of drum rhythms should be played. Not only can you hear each one, but you can see which particular drum should be played at each moment by studying the notes across the top and comparing these to the indications next to each drum. To further aid the student, the tempo can be altered, allowing the student to practice at their own pace.
