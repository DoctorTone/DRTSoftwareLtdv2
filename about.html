About Us

We create highly interactive 3D experiences.

Who We Are

DRT was formed in 2015 with the aim of providing freelance software development, within the visualisation and medical imaging industries. Since then we have created all types of 3D applications embracing data visualisation, surgical simulation, collaborative gaming, learning environments, virtual scenario design and much more.

We are passionate about creating 3D experiences and our goal is to create engaging applications that people love to use.

We are a small company and as such we can provide a more flexible and personal approach. We are focused on our areas of expertise and this leads to greater customer engagement and satisfaction.

We are based in Nottingham, England.

What We Do

We love all things 3D!

We believe experiences are enhanced by positioning them in 3D space. Imagine visualising your traditional 2D data in a 3D environment. or running through a virtual building, simulating complex operations, examining 3D structures as if they were in front of you. We take your data and ideas and create an engaging and visually stunning environment for you to interact and explore.

We engage with you through every step of the process and ensure everything gets done, no fuss, no hassle, no problems.

Dr Tony Glover

Director

Tony founded the company in 2015 and has more than 30 years experience in the software development industry. He has worked in the commercial and academic sectors in fields including computer graphics, simulation and medical imaging.
