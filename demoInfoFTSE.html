FTSE Visualisation

The financial sector generates a huge quantity of data and therefore represents ideal visualisation and analysis opportunities. This visualisation attempts to convey multiple facets of the data - each collection of segments shows the variations on a daily or weekly basis throughout the year. The height of each column is also proportional to the yearly maximum, thereby giving an indication as to where the FTSE stood in relation to its best value at any given time. This is an update of a previous financial visualisation incorporating an updated design.
